About This Site · 关于本站
家庭、企业、政府——
人类社会三种最基本的有组织力量，
如何才能不相互碾压，而是相互成就？

本站围绕这一问题，提出"合三为一政治经济学"框架，
以"生命力高于生产力"为核心命题，
研究家庭权利、企业权利、政府权力
“大三权”分立与非对称制衡机制的制度逻辑。

在这个框架下，本站尝试回答：
为什么市场经济在带来繁荣的同时，
制造了生育率崩溃、贫富分化、有效需求不足？
AI革命正在以何种方式颠覆既有的分配机制和社会组织结构？
人类文明在AGI奇点临近时，出路在哪里？

这里没有现成答案，只有认真的追问。
Family, Enterprise, and State — the three fundamental organized forces of human society. How can they sustain rather than devour one another?

This site develops the framework of "Synthesis Political Economy", guided by a central proposition: Life-Force stands above Productive Force. It examines the institutional logic of the "Grand Tripartite" — the asymmetric balance among Family Rights, Enterprise Rights, and State Power.

Within this framework, the site asks: Why does market prosperity coexist with collapsing birth rates, widening inequality, and chronic demand deficiency? How is the AI revolution disrupting established mechanisms of distribution and structures of social organization? And where does human civilization turn as the AGI Singularity approaches?

No ready answers here — only serious inquiry.
